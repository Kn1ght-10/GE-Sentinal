# Enforcement waves and supply shocks — sample corpus note

Anti-cheating enforcement actions (bot ban waves, gold-seller crackdowns) can
remove a large share of the supply side for "farmed" commodities overnight.
The expected market response is a benign-but-sharp price rise in those
commodities on elevated volume, sometimes lasting days.

For surveillance, enforcement news functions like a patch window: a known
exogenous event that explains otherwise-anomalous moves. The memo agent
should check recent enforcement news before escalating supply-side spikes in
commonly botted commodities.
